Flan

Flan is a traditional Spanish dessert made with eggs, sugar, and milk. It has a silky texture similar to custard and is topped with caramel.

Ingredients

  • Whole milk, 500 ml
  • Eggs, 4
  • Sugar, 150 g (100 g for the corpus, 50 g for caramel)
  • Vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon

Recipe

  1. Prepare the caramel by melting 50 g of sugar mixed with water in a pan until golden brown. Immediately pour it into the base of your flan mold.
  2. Heat the milk in a pot until warm (do not boil). Stir in vanilla extract.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs with 100 g sugar until smooth.
  4. Slowly pour the warm milk into the egg mixture while whisking to avoid curdling.
  5. Pour the mixture into the caramel-coated molds.
  6. Place the molds in a baking dish and fill the dish with hot water until halfway up the molds.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 160°C for about 50–60 minutes, until set.
  8. Put it into the fridge over night, then invert onto plates before serving.
