Cookies

Cookies are a classic dessert that can be prepared quickly with simple ingredients. They are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour, 250 g
  • Butter (room temperature), 125 g
  • Sugar, 100 g
  • Brown sugar, 50 g
  • Egg, 1
  • Vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon
  • Baking powder, 1 teaspoon
  • Salt, a pinch
  • Chocolate chips, 150 g

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  7. Using a spoon, scoop small portions of dough onto the baking tray, leaving space between cookies.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden around the edges but still soft in the center.
  9. Allow to cool on a rack before serving.
